Feidong(Henry) Song

About Me

Feidong (Henry) Song, a dedicated student at the University of California, Berkeley, is pursuing dual majors in Computer Science and Data Science. His entrepreneurial spirit is evident from his ventures: founding GPTBang, Inc., a platform harnessing the capabilities of the ChatGPT API and recognized by the CSAC 2023 Spring accelerator program, and co-founding CoffeeTea, Inc., an innovative online marketplace for college students. As a Software Engineer Intern at WanderFlow, Henry is enhancing mobile application responsiveness and is at the forefront of developing Realcollegeranking.com, a platform set to redefine college rankings. With a keen interest in LLM and its applications, he's actively seeking research opportunities to further expand his academic horizons. Beyond his professional and academic pursuits, Henry's roots trace back to the artistic hub of Chongqing, China, where he was born into an art-loving family. His personal interests also include basketball, having played for one of Canada's top high school teams, showcasing his diverse talents and experiences.
